FAQs

Quick answers for the most common homeowner questions.

When are HOA dues due?

Dues can be paid by check annually or bi-annually. Current HOA dues $600/year.

Please make checks payable to: Clifton Grove HOA

How do I submit an Architectural Review (ARC) request?

Placeholder: Download the ARC form, attach photos/specs, and email to arc@cliftongrove.org.

Who do I contact about landscaping or common areas?

Placeholder: Contact the Property Manager or Board Liaison at landscape@cliftongrove.org.

Where can I find governing documents and bylaws?

Placeholder: Link your CC&Rs, bylaws, rules & regulations, and meeting minutes in the “Useful Links” section below.

What’s the policy for trash/recycling pickup?

Disposal is handled by Patriot Disposal Services. Please visit their site for schedule changes due to weather & holidays.

Trash is picked up every Tuesday and Friday, recycling is picked up every Wednesday (please do not include glass). Place bins curbside the night before.

How do I report a neighborhood concern?

Non-emergency: email board@cliftongrove.org with photos/details. Emergency: call 911.
